Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about Excellence University

How do I apply for admission?
▼

You can apply for admission through our online application portal. Create an account, fill out the application form, upload required documents, and pay the application fee. Our admissions team will review your application and contact you within 2-3 weeks.

What are the tuition fees?
▼

Tuition fees vary by program and level of study. For undergraduate programs, tuition ranges from $10,000 to $15,000 per semester. Graduate programs range from $12,000 to $18,000 per semester. Financial aid and scholarship opportunities are available for eligible students.

Do you offer online courses?
▼

Yes, we offer a variety of online and hybrid courses across many programs. Our online learning platform provides flexibility for students who need to balance studies with other commitments. Check our programs page for specific online offerings.

How can I schedule a campus tour?
▼

You can schedule a campus tour by contacting our admissions office at (02) 2889-3416 or by filling out the campus tour request form on our website. Tours are available Monday through Friday at 10:00 AM and 2:00 PM, and on select Saturdays.

What housing options are available?
▼

We offer on-campus housing in our residence halls and apartment-style accommodations. Off-campus housing assistance is also available through our housing office. Applications for on-campus housing should be submitted by May 1st for the fall semester.
